About Me

Taruna is an advocate practicing before the Supreme Court of India, the Delhi High Court, and various tribunals including the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) and the National Company Law Appellate Tribunal (NCLAT). Her areas of expertise include Commercial and Company Litigation, Alternative Dispute Resolution, NDPS laws, and Taxation. She has represented clients and argued matters across multiple judicial and quasi-judicial forums, including the Supreme Court, Delhi High Court, NCLT, NCLAT, and other tribunals and commissions across India.

My practice areas include Insolvency and Bankruptcy Law, GST and Taxation, NDPS Law, Arbitration and Alternative Dispute Resolution, and Commercial and Company Law.
